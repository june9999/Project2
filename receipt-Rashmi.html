Dhokla is a soft and fluffy steamed chickpea flour cake from Gujarat, western India. This airy, sweet and sour cake is prepared with spices, freshly-grated coconut and coriander leaves.

Ingredients for Dhokla

Gram flour (besan) 2 cups Yogurt whisked 1 cup Salt to taste
Green chillies 2-3 Ginger 1 1/2 inch piece Turmeric powder 1/2 TS
Soda bicarbonate 1 TS Lemon juice 1 TS Mustard seeds 1 TS
Fresh coriander leaves chopped 2 TS Coconut scraped 1/4 cup Cumin seeds 1 TS
