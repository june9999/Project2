How To Make The Best Chicken

In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt (if using), Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine and create the dressing base. Add the shredded or diced chicken to the bowl and mix it with the dressing until it is well coated. Add the celery, red onion, sweet pickle relish (or chopped pickles), and any optional add-ins you desire. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients. Taste the chicken salad and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. You can add more salt, pepper, or lemon juice if needed. Once the chicken salad is thoroughly mixed, cover the bowl and refrigerate it for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve the chicken salad on a bed of lettuce, in a sandwich, or with your choice of sides. It can be enjoyed immediately or stored in the refrigerator for up to three days. Feel free to customize this basic chicken salad recipe to suit your taste preferences by adding or omitting ingredients. Enjoy your homemade chicken salad!
